Welcome to Little Lemon

Little Lemon is a small company that specializes in making lemonade. We have been in business for over 10 years and have been making the best lemonade in the world. We have a variety of flavors and we are always coming up with new ones. We are a family owned business and we take pride in our product. We hope you enjoy our lemonade as much as we do.

Our Flavors

Our flavors are made with the freshest lemons and the best ingredients. We have a variety of flavors to choose from. We have the classic lemonade, strawberry lemonade, blueberry lemonade, and many more. We are always coming up with new flavors so be sure to check back often.

Our Story

Little Lemon was started by a family who loved lemonade. They wanted to share their love of lemonade with the world. They started making lemonade in their kitchen and selling it at local farmers markets. They quickly became popular and decided to start their own business. They have been making lemonade for over 10 years and have been growing ever since.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the best lemonade in the world. We take pride in our product and we are always looking for ways to improve. We want to share our love of lemonade with the world and we hope you enjoy it as much as we do.
